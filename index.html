Addition

One of the four basic math operations alongside:
Subtraction, multiplication and division.

Addition can be used to combine the values of two or more separate numbers.

For example:
2 + 3 = 5
In this case, 2 and 3 have been added together to make 5.

Sometimes, maths questions will ask you something like "calculate the sum of 2 and 3".

This type of question is just asking you, "what is the result of 2 + 3?"
So the 'sum' of 2 and 3 is 5.
